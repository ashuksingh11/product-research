## 1. Haier Brand: SmartSense AI Technology

### 1.1 What Is SmartSense AI?

**SmartSense AI** is Haier's branded AI feature for its refrigerator line, primarily deployed in the Indian and Asian markets. It is an adaptive cooling intelligence system that uses sensor fusion and machine learning to optimize refrigerator performance in real time.

**Core Mechanism:**
SmartSense AI continuously monitors three categories of real-time data:
1. **Door activity** -- Frequency and duration of door openings
2. **Environmental conditions** -- Ambient temperature fluctuations outside the refrigerator
3. **Food storage patterns** -- The quantity and type of stored food items

Based on this sensor data, the AI system dynamically determines the refrigerator's optimal cooling needs, adjusting compressor cycles and airflow to maintain ideal preservation conditions without manual user intervention.

**Adaptive Behavior:**
- Intensifies cooling during meal preparation periods when doors are opened frequently
- Reduces power consumption during inactive nighttime hours
- Prevents unnecessary overcooling and energy waste
- Learns user habits over time to predict and pre-adjust settings

**Energy Impact:**
Haier claims SmartSense AI delivers **up to 30% energy savings** compared to conventional refrigerators with fixed cooling cycles.

**Additional Smart Features Tied to SmartSense AI:**

### 1.3 SmartSense AI: Technical Observations

SmartSense AI operates as an **on-device (edge) system**. The sensor data is processed locally on the refrigerator's embedded controller, making it function independently of cloud connectivity. WiFi-enabled models extend the system by allowing remote monitoring and adjustment through the HaiSmart app, but the core AI logic runs locally.

### 4.1 CookCam AI (In-Oven Camera + Food Recognition)

**What It Is:**
CookCam AI is an AI-powered food identification and automated cooking system built into select GE Profile and CAFE wall ovens equipped with in-oven cameras.

**How It Works (Technical Flow):**
1. User places food in the oven and closes the door
2. The in-oven camera captures images of the oven cavity
3. AI and machine learning algorithms analyze the images to **detect and identify the food**
4. Within seconds, the oven chimes and recommends a specific **Precision Cooking Mode** on the LCD touchscreen
5. Once the user accepts, the oven **automatically tracks progress, modifies temperatures, and adjusts cook times** throughout the cooking process

**Food Recognition (Current Scope):**
As of the initial release, CookCam AI recognizes **five popular food categories**:
- Turkey
- Cookies
- Pizza
- Cakes
- Brownies

GE has confirmed plans to expand recognized food types through future OTA software updates.

**Precision Cooking Modes:**
These modes have been developed by GE's food scientists through **thousands of tests** to ensure reliable results without manual monitoring. Each mode automatically controls temperature curves and timing for its specific food type.

**Privacy Architecture (CRITICAL DETAIL -- Edge AI):**
**"These images stay on the device, not in the cloud."**
CookCam AI uses **on-device (edge) processing** for all image capture and food identification. No visual data is transmitted to cloud servers. This is a significant privacy differentiator compared to cloud-dependent vision systems.

**Deployment Method:**
CookCam AI is delivered as an **over-the-air (OTA) software upgrade** to existing owners of compatible wall ovens, meaning consumers do not need to purchase new hardware.

**Compatible Models:**

**Additional Capability:**
The built-in camera also enables **remote monitoring** through the SmartHQ app, eliminating the need to open the oven door to check cooking progress. CookCam also stores cooking history.

**Pricing:**
New GE Profile and CAFE wall oven models with CookCam capability start at **$3,499 MSRP**, available at retailers nationwide starting **July 2026**.

### 4.3 FridgeFocus Camera (AI-Powered Crisper Drawer Vision)

**What It Is:**
FridgeFocus is an AI-powered internal camera system designed to address the common problem of overbuying perishables and forgetting refrigerator contents.

**How It Works:**
- Integrated into a **flush-mount LED bar** inside the refrigerator
- Delivers **real-time, on-demand snapshots** of crisper drawers
- Specifically focuses on the **most costly and perishable items** (produce, fresh items)
- AI-powered vision system enables anyone in the household to **remotely check inventory while shopping** via the SmartHQ app
- Helps prevent duplicate purchases and food waste

**Privacy Controls:**
- Users can **disable the camera via the SmartHQ app**
- A **physical shutter slide** provides hardware-level camera blocking

**Key Differentiator:**
Unlike Samsung's AI Vision Inside (which captures full refrigerator interior images), FridgeFocus specifically targets crisper drawers where the highest-value perishables are stored, providing a more focused and practical use case.

### 4.4 Flavorly AI (Generative AI Recipe Generator)

**What It Is:**
Flavorly AI is GE Appliances' generative AI recipe creation engine, integrated into the SmartHQ app. It is one of the earliest consumer-facing generative AI features in the appliance industry, initially launched in **August 2023**.

**Technology Stack:**
- Powered by **Google Cloud's Vertex AI** platform
- Uses **Google Gemini AI models** for multimodal processing (images + text)
- Available in the **SmartHQ app** on both Android and iOS

**How It Works:**
1. User selects a **recipe category** and **type of cuisine**
2. User inputs **available ingredients** (via text, voice, or photo)
3. User specifies **dietary preferences** or restrictions
4. Flavorly AI generates **tailored recipes** including:
   - Complete ingredient lists
   - Step-by-step preparation instructions
   - AI-generated recipe photos
5. Users can **rate recipes** to improve the AI model over time (rating feature added Fall 2023)

**2025 Enhancements -- Visual Recognition:**
- Users can **snap a photo of their refrigerator contents** and instantly get personalized recipe suggestions
- **Photo-to-recipe**: Take a photo of a favorite restaurant dish, and Flavorly AI will reverse-engineer a recipe
- **Handwritten recipe digitization**: Photograph handwritten family recipes, and the AI converts them to digital format with adjustments
- **Integrated grocery shopping**: Recipes can be converted to shopping lists and synced with **Instacart's network**

**Accessibility:**
Flavorly AI is available to **anyone with the SmartHQ app**, even without owning a connected GE Appliance. This is a deliberate strategy to drive SmartHQ app adoption.

**Note on "40,000+ Ingredients":**
While this figure has been cited in some industry analyses, GE Appliances' official press releases reference the system's ability to process user-input ingredients of any type through the generative AI model rather than citing a specific fixed ingredient database size. The generative AI approach means the system can handle an effectively unlimited range of ingredients.

## 6. Haier's Approach: Edge AI vs. Cloud AI

Haier employs a **hybrid approach** across its brand portfolio, with a notable emphasis on edge AI for privacy-sensitive features.

### 6.1 Edge AI (On-Device Processing)

| Feature/Product | Edge AI Implementation | Privacy Benefit |
|----------------|----------------------|-----------------|
| **CookCam AI** (GE Profile) | All food images processed on-device; "images stay on the device, not in the cloud" | No visual data leaves the oven |
| **HomeGPT** (Haier) | Level 4 certified edge-based LLM; provides offline intelligent services without cloud | Full functionality without internet; strong data protection |
| **SmartSense AI** (Haier) | Sensor data processed by local embedded controller | Core cooling optimization runs independently of WiFi |
| **AI Vision washing machine** (Haier) | Camera + 9 sensors process garment data locally for cycle optimization | Garment images not transmitted to cloud |

### 6.2 Cloud AI (Server-Side Processing)

| Feature/Product | Cloud AI Implementation | Technology Partner |
|----------------|------------------------|-------------------|
| **Flavorly AI** (GE Appliances) | Recipe generation via Google Cloud Vertex AI / Gemini models | Google Cloud |
| **SmartHQ AI Assistants** (GE) | Conversational AI for coffee, laundry, kitchen guidance | Google Cloud generative AI |
| **DeepSeek Integration** (Haier China) | LLM integration for enhanced smart home interactions | DeepSeek |
| **FridgeFocus** (GE Profile) | Image snapshots transmitted to SmartHQ app for remote viewing | GE Appliances cloud |
| **Scan-to-List** (GE Profile) | Barcode data synced to SmartHQ app and Instacart | GE Appliances cloud + Instacart |

### 6.3 Strategic Positioning

Haier's approach reveals a clear philosophy:
- **Privacy-sensitive visual data** (oven images, laundry camera) stays on-device through edge AI
- **Generative AI features** (recipe creation, conversational assistants) leverage cloud computing through Google Cloud partnership
- **Core appliance functions** (cooling optimization, wash cycle adaptation) run on edge for reliability and offline functionality
- **HomeGPT as the edge AI centerpiece**: By achieving Level 4 CAICT certification for edge-based operation, Haier positions itself as a leader in privacy-respecting on-device AI for the home
